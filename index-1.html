Welcome to Studio Archigram

Welcome to
Studio Archigram

Studio Archigram is based out of Nashik, India. Studio Archigram is a design practice and a collective of creative thinkers. As designers we recognize and respect our role in impacting life, be it residential interior design, commercial interior design, hospitality interior design or interior design for multi use spaces.
We specialize in creating beautiful, functional, and personalized spaces. Located in Nashik, our team of skilled architects and interior designers is dedicated to transforming your ideas into reality. Whether you're looking to redesign your home interiors, plan a new architectural project, or create a stylish modular kitchen, we provide comprehensive solutions tailored to your needs.
